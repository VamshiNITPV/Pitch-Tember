About Event

What is PitchTember ?

The National Institute of Technology Patna is hosting Pitchtember Fest, an event focused on empowering entrepreneurs. The event follows a journey from awakening to refinement, offering a comprehensive entrepreneurial experience.
Udhyamita:This day is a dynamic blend of entrepreneurship and skill development. Participants will learn to fuse their newly acquired skills with innovative ideas, laying the foundation for their entrepreneurial journey.
Unnati: It is all about pitching ideas and attracting investment. It's a spotlight on innovation, where participants present their entrepreneurial dreams to potential investors and mentors.
Uthaan:Day one sets the stage for an inspiring journey. Participants will awaken their inner entrepreneurs, as they delve into the world of creativity, innovation, and idea generation
Unnayan:As we progress, Day focuses on skill development and growth. It's all about nurturing and enhancing the abilities that budding entrepreneurs need to succeed.
Upsanskaran:The grand finale of Pitchtember is a celebration of innovation and entrepreneurial excellence. Participants showcase how far they've come, as the event culminates with awards and recognition.
